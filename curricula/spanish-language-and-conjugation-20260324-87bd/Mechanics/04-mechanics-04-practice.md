## Exercises

**Exercise 1**
Conjugate the verb *querer* (e->ie, to want) for the subject pronoun *ella*.

**Exercise 2**
Complete the sentence with the correct present tense form of the verb *encontrar* (o->ue, to find): "Yo no __________ mis llaves."

**Exercise 3**
A student wrote the sentence: "Nosotros *podemos* ir al cine, pero vosotros no *puedeis*." One of these conjugations of the verb *poder* (o->ue) is incorrect. Identify the incorrect form and explain the rule that was broken.

**Exercise 4**
The verb *defender* (to defend) is an e->ie stem-changing verb. Based on this information, how would you conjugate it for the subject *tú*? Explain the two steps you would take to arrive at the correct form.

**Exercise 5**
Complete the following dialogue by conjugating the verbs in parentheses. One verb is a regular -ER verb, and two are stem-changing.

A: ¿A qué hora (cerrar, e->ie) __________ la biblioteca?
B: (Abrir, regular -IR) __________ a las 8 de la mañana y (cerrar) __________ a las 9 de la noche.
A: Gracias. ¿Tú (pensar, e->ie) __________ ir hoy?

**Exercise 6**
Imagine you are a server in a restaurant. A customer is deciding what to order. Ask them a question using the verb *preferir* (e->ie) and then tell them about the daily special using the verb *costar* (o->ue). Write these two sentences in Spanish.

---

## Answer Key

**Answer 1**
The correct conjugation is **quiere**.

**Reasoning:**
The verb *querer* is an e->ie stem-changing verb.
1.  The stem is *quer-*. For the *ella* form, the 'e' in the stem changes to 'ie', becoming *quier-*.
2.  The standard -ER ending for *ella* is '-e'.
3.  Combining the changed stem and the ending gives *quiere*.

**Answer 2**
The correct form is **encuentro**. The complete sentence is "Yo no **encuentro** mis llaves."

**Reasoning:**
The verb *encontrar* is an o->ue stem-changing verb.
1.  The stem is *encontr-*. For the *yo* form, the 'o' in the stem changes to 'ue', becoming *encuentr-*.
2.  The standard -AR ending for *yo* is '-o'.
3.  Combining the changed stem and the ending gives *encuentro*.

**Answer 3**
The incorrect form is *puedeis*. It should be **podéis**.

**Reasoning:**
Stem-changing verbs do not change their stem in the *nosotros* (we) and *vosotros* (you all, informal) forms. The verb *poder* correctly remains *podemos* for *nosotros*. However, the student incorrectly applied the o->ue stem change to the *vosotros* form. It should follow the regular -ER verb conjugation pattern for *vosotros*, which is to use the original stem (*pod-*) and add the ending *-éis*.

**Answer 4**
The correct conjugation is **defiendes**.

**Reasoning:**
1.  **Stem Change:** The verb *defender* is an e->ie stem-changing verb. For the *tú* form, the stem *defend-* changes to *defiend-*.
2.  **Add Ending:** The verb is an -ER verb. The correct present tense ending for the subject *tú* is *-es*.
3.  **Combine:** Combining the new stem (*defiend-*) and the ending (*-es*) results in *defiendes*.

**Answer 5**
The completed dialogue is:

A: ¿A qué hora **cierra** la biblioteca?
B: **Abre** a las 8 de la mañana y **cierra** a las 9 de la noche.
A: Gracias. ¿Tú **piensas** ir hoy?

**Reasoning:**
-   *cerrar* (subject: *la biblioteca* -> *ella*) is an e->ie stem-changing verb, so it becomes **cierra**.
-   *abrir* (subject: *la biblioteca* -> *ella*) is a regular -IR verb (from a prior lesson), so it uses the standard ending: **abre**.
-   *pensar* (subject: *tú*) is an e->ie stem-changing verb, so it becomes **piensas**.

This exercise required identifying which verbs were stem-changing and which were regular before applying the correct conjugation rules.

**Answer 6**
There are multiple correct answers, but here is a model response.

Question: **"¿Qué prefiere usted comer?"** (What do you prefer to eat?)
Statement: **"El especial del día cuesta quince dólares."** (The daily special costs fifteen dollars.)

**Reasoning:**
-   For the question, *preferir* (e->ie) is conjugated for the formal *usted* subject. The stem *prefer-* changes to *prefier-*, and the -IR ending for *usted* is '-e', resulting in **prefiere**.
-   For the statement, *costar* (o->ue) is conjugated for the subject *el especial del día* (it). The stem *cost-* changes to *cuest-*, and the -AR ending for *él/ella/usted* is '-a', resulting in **cuesta**.
