## Exercises

**Exercise 1**
Your boss sent an email asking everyone to bring their project reports to the meeting. To confirm you understand, you reply with the following sentence. Complete the sentence with the correct subjunctive form of *traer*.

"Gracias. Es necesario que nosotros ________ (traer) los informes a la reunión."

**Exercise 2**
You are giving advice to a friend who is traveling to another country. You want them to be aware of their surroundings. Complete your recommendation using the correct subjunctive form of *estar*.

"Te aconsejo que ________ (estar) atento/a en los lugares turísticos."

**Exercise 3**
A student has written two possible sentences to express their desire for their parents to give them a new computer. Only one is grammatically correct.

A) "Quiero que mis padres me *dan* una computadora nueva."
B) "Quiero que mis padres me *den* una computadora nueva."

Which sentence is correct? Explain the specific error in the incorrect sentence by referencing the rules for forming the present subjunctive of the verb *dar*.

**Exercise 4**
Analyze the following sentence, which contains a common error related to stem-changing verbs in the subjunctive:

"El profesor no quiere que nosotros *piensamos* que el examen es fácil."

Identify the conjugation error and provide the corrected sentence. Explain the rule for stem-changing `-ar` and `-er` verbs in the present subjunctive that was not correctly applied here.

**Exercise 5**
Imagine this scenario: Yesterday, your friend told you they didn't understand the homework (*Ayer, tu amigo te dijo que no entendía la tarea.*). Today, you want to express your hope that they are able to ask the teacher for help.

Construct a new, single sentence that connects this past situation to your present hope. Your sentence must start with a phrase like "Espero que..." or "Ojalá que..." and correctly use the subjunctive form of *poder*.

**Exercise 6**
You are a manager leaving instructions for your team before you go on vacation. You need to express a firm, impersonal requirement that the weekly report *is* on your desk when you return. You must use the verb *ser*.

Write a complete, original sentence that accomplishes this. Your sentence should begin with an impersonal expression (e.g., "Es crucial que...", "Es imprescindible que...") and demonstrate correct use of the irregular subjunctive form of *ser*.

## Answer Key

**Answer 1**
The correct sentence is: "Gracias. Es necesario que nosotros **traigamos** los informes a la reunión."

**Reasoning:**
1.  **Trigger:** The phrase "Es necesario que..." is an impersonal expression, which is one of the WEIRDO triggers requiring the subjunctive mood.
2.  **Verb Formation:** The verb *traer* is irregular. To form the present subjunctive, you start with the first-person singular ("yo") form of the present indicative, which is *traigo*.
3.  **Conjugation:** You then drop the "-o" to get the stem *traig-* and add the appropriate opposite vowel ending for *nosotros* ("-amos"). This results in *traigamos*.

**Answer 2**
The correct sentence is: "Te aconsejo que **estés** atento/a en los lugares turísticos."

**Reasoning:**
1.  **Trigger:** The phrase "Te aconsejo que..." is a recommendation, which requires the subjunctive.
2.  **Verb Formation:** *Estar* is one of the highly irregular "DISHES" verbs. Its subjunctive forms do not follow a standard pattern and must be memorized.
3.  **Conjugation:** The correct second-person singular ("tú") form is *estés*, which includes both the irregular stem and a written accent to maintain the correct stress.

**Answer 3**
Sentence B is correct: "Quiero que mis padres me **den** una computadora nueva."

**Reasoning:**
The error in sentence A ("dan") is the use of the indicative mood instead of the subjunctive.
1.  **Trigger:** The main clause "Quiero que..." expresses a wish or desire, triggering the subjunctive in the subordinate clause.
2.  **Verb Formation:** The verb *dar* is a highly irregular "DISHES" verb. Its present indicative form (*dan*) cannot be used here.
3.  **Correct Conjugation:** The correct third-person plural ("ellos/mis padres") present subjunctive form of *dar* is *den*.

**Answer 4**
The error is the conjugation of *pensar*. The corrected sentence is: "El profesor no quiere que nosotros **pensemos** que el examen es fácil."

**Reasoning:**
The verb *pensar* is a stem-changing verb (*e* -> *ie*). However, this stem change does not apply to the *nosotros* and *vosotros* forms in the present subjunctive.
1.  **Rule:** For `-ar` and `-er` stem-changing verbs, the stem change occurs in all forms *except* the first-person plural (*nosotros*) and second-person plural (*vosotros*).
2.  **Application:** The student incorrectly applied the *ie* stem change to the *nosotros* form, creating "*piensamos*". The correct form, *pensemos*, reverts to the original stem *pens-*.

**Answer 5**
A correct sentence would be: "Ojalá que mi amigo **pueda** preguntarle a la profesora hoy." (Other variations are possible, but the key structure must be present).

**Reasoning:**
This exercise requires synthesizing a past event with a present hope.
1.  **Tense/Mood Integration:** The past event (*dijo*) sets up the context, but the expression of hope ("Ojalá que...") requires a shift to the present subjunctive mood for the following verb.
2.  **Verb Formation:** *Poder* is an *o* -> *ue* stem-changing verb. Its "yo" form in the present indicative is *puedo*.
3.  **Conjugation:** To form the subjunctive for "él/ella" (tu amigo), you use the stem *pued-* and add the opposite ending "-a," resulting in *pueda*.

**Answer 6**
A correct sentence would be: "Es imprescindible que el informe semanal **sea** entregado antes del viernes." or "Es crucial que el informe **sea** el primer documento en mi escritorio cuando regrese."

**Reasoning:**
This task requires generating a complete sentence that reflects a realistic professional context.
1.  **Trigger Choice:** An impersonal expression of necessity like "Es imprescindible que..." or "Es crucial que..." strongly triggers the subjunctive.
2.  **Verb Choice:** The prompt required the verb *ser* to express the state of the report *being* on the desk.
3.  **Irregular Conjugation:** *Ser* is a highly irregular "DISHES" verb. The third-person singular ("el informe") form in the present subjunctive is *sea*. The resulting sentence is formal, clear, and grammatically correct.
